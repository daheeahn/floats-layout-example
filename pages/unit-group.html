Even of Day

Even of Day is the first sub-unit of the popular South Korean band Day6, debuting on August 31, 2020, under JYP Entertainment. The unit consists of three members: Young K, Wonpil, and Dowoon. Known for their distinct emotional depth, Even of Day brings a unique sound that sets them apart within the Day6 universe. Their debut album, The Book of Us: Gluon, received praise for its introspective lyrics and intricate musical compositions, solidifying their place as a standout unit in the K-pop scene.

Even of Day, a sub-unit of the renowned South Korean band Day6, debuted on August 31, 2020. The unit features members Young K, Wonpil, and Dowoon, each bringing their own musical expertise and emotional depth to the group. Even of Day stands out for its unique sound, blending the signature Day6 style with more introspective and melodic elements. Their debut album, The Book of Us: Gluon, was well-received, showcasing their ability to create music that resonates deeply with listeners. With heartfelt lyrics and intricate arrangements, Even of Day has carved out a distinct place within the K-pop scene, appealing to both long-time Day6 fans and new audiences alike.
